Hack Your School's Website!

In this Maker Party, you'll remix your school's web site. It's fun, creative, and lets you mess around with HTML, the language that makes up the web. It's easy! Don't worry -- you're not actually hacking the site for others. You're changing a version of the site that only you can see.

Once you drag this to your browser's toolbar, you will have the power of X-Ray vision on any website you visit!
